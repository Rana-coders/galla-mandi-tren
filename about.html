GHALLA MANDI PAKISTAN

Pakistan's real-time agricultural commodity price dashboard — bringing transparency to farmers, traders, and buyers across the country.

Real-Time Prices

Live commodity prices updated every 10 seconds, scraped from trusted Pakistani market sources daily.

City-Wise Rates

Detailed mandi rates for 50+ cities across Punjab, Sindh, KPK and Balochistan — per 40kg (1 Mann).

International Futures

CBOT Wheat and CME Cotton futures with live USD/PKR conversion to help understand global market impact.

Free & Open

Completely free to use. No registration required. Works on mobile, tablet and desktop.
